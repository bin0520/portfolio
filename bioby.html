I had the exciting opportunity to work closely with the founding team of Bioby.ai to shape the platform from the ground up. Bioby.ai is a SaaS startup providing database and CRM tools built to streamline influencer-brand collaborations, with a focus on simplifying outreach, managing deals, and ensuring secure, transparent payments.

In this project, I led the design of the company's visual identity, including the logo design and the public-facing website. This work established the foundation for Bioby.ai's brand presence and set the stage for future internal SaaS platform design.

Timeframe

Ongoing project

As the Product Lead, I worked closely with the founders, development team, marketing team, and operations team to establish Bioby.ai's brand identity and design the public website that would serve as the company's digital front door.

Project Overview

Bioby.ai needed a strong visual identity to establish credibility in the competitive influencer marketing space. The project involved two main components:

  • Logo Design: Creating a distinctive logo that represents the brand's mission of connecting creators and brands
  • Website Design: Designing a modern, conversion-focused website that clearly communicates Bioby.ai's value proposition to both creators and brands

These foundational design elements would not only serve the public website but also inform the design direction for the internal SaaS platform that would follow.

Logo Design

The logo design process began with understanding Bioby.ai's core mission: to create meaningful connections between creators and brands. I explored various concepts that would communicate partnership, collaboration, and the dynamic nature of influencer marketing.

The final logo features two stylized bird silhouettes facing each other‚Äîone in black, one in white‚Äîcreating a balanced, symmetrical composition. This design symbolizes the two-sided marketplace that Bioby.ai facilitates, where creators and brands come together in partnership. The birds represent freedom, creativity, and the ability to soar, while their facing orientation emphasizes connection and collaboration.

The Bioby.ai logo design featuring two complementary bird silhouettes

Design Considerations

  • Versatility: The logo works across various applications‚Äîfrom website headers to social media profiles
  • Scalability: The simple, geometric forms ensure clarity at any size
  • Memorability: The distinctive bird motif creates a memorable brand symbol
  • Balance: The symmetrical design conveys harmony and partnership

Website Design

The website design needed to serve multiple audiences: creators looking for brand partnerships and brands seeking the right creators for their campaigns. The design emphasizes clarity, trust, and ease of use.

Website mockups showcasing the complete design system

Key Design Features

  • Hero Section: Clear value proposition with prominent call-to-action buttons
  • Feature Pages: Dedicated pages for Email, Order Management, Client Management, and Payment features
  • Service Pages: Separate sections for Creators and Brands, each tailored to their specific needs
  • Visual Consistency: Cohesive design system using a vibrant green accent color for interactive elements
  • Trust Building: Partner logos and testimonials to establish credibility

Design System

The website uses a clean, modern aesthetic with:

  • Color Palette: White backgrounds, gray/black text, and a bright green (#00FF00 or similar) for CTAs and highlights
  • Typography: Clean, readable fonts that maintain professionalism while feeling approachable
  • Layout: Consistent grid system with clear visual hierarchy
  • Components: Reusable UI components that maintain consistency across all pages
Detailed views of key website features and pages

Design Process

The design process involved close collaboration with stakeholders to ensure the visual identity accurately represented Bioby.ai's mission and values. Key steps included:

  1. Discovery: Understanding the brand positioning, target audiences, and competitive landscape
  2. Concept Development: Exploring multiple logo concepts and website layout options
  3. Iteration: Refining designs based on feedback from founders and key stakeholders
  4. Implementation: Creating final assets and design specifications for development

Future Work

This project established the foundation for Bioby.ai's visual identity. The next phase involves designing the internal SaaS platform, which will extend the design system created for the website into a comprehensive CRM interface that supports the day-to-day workflows of creators and brands.

Takeaways

Leading the design of Bioby.ai's brand identity and website was an exciting challenge that required balancing multiple stakeholder needs while creating a cohesive visual system. The logo and website design not only established the company's public presence but also set the design direction for future product development. This project reinforced the importance of creating a strong foundation‚Äîboth visually and strategically‚Äîthat can scale as the company grows.
