At a Glance

I designed and developed a modular web application that allows clients to log in and manage their marketing operations autonomously. The portal focuses on transparency and automation, serving as a centralized "Brand Portal" to unify overseas influencer marketing operations into a single client dashboard.

The platform serves as a single source of truth for clients, automating the journey from influencer selection to financial settlement, while providing real-time visibility into campaign status, budget burn rate, and influencer selection progress.

Key Modules
  • Dashboard: High-level command center with active campaigns, total spend, and aggregate reach statistics
  • Campaign Manager: Kanban-style view tracking collaboration status (Sourcing → Content Creation → Posted)
  • Influencer Database: Searchable directory with performance metrics and portfolio samples
  • Finance & Reporting: Automated invoices and downloadable performance reports

Discover

The Challenge

Our agency specializes in connecting brands with overseas Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs). However, our internal workflow and client reporting were fragmented.

The Disconnect

Client data was scattered across spreadsheets, WeChat/WhatsApp logs, and manual PDF reports.

The Friction

Clients had no real-time visibility into their campaign status, budget burn rate, or influencer selection progress.

The Goal

Build a centralized "Brand Portal" to serve as a single source of truth for clients, automating the journey from influencer selection to financial settlement.

Define

The Solution

I designed and developed a modular web application that allows clients to log in and manage their marketing operations autonomously. The portal focuses on transparency and automation.

Dashboard

High-level command center showing active campaigns, total spend, and aggregate reach statistics

Campaign Manager

Kanban-style view where clients can track collaboration status (Sourcing → Content Creation → Posted)

Influencer Database

Searchable directory of overseas KOLs, complete with performance metrics and portfolio samples

Finance & Reporting

Automated module that generates invoices and downloadable performance reports

Develop

My Contribution

Because I straddled the line between product management and development, I was able to ensure the design was both feasible and user-centric.

Product Management

I gathered requirements from internal stakeholders to define the user stories for the Finance and Reporting modules. I prioritized the roadmap to focus on high-value features first, such as automated status updates.

Front-End Development

I built the core interface using React. My focus was on creating a polished, high-end "Liquid Glass" aesthetic to match the creative nature of our industry. I also focused heavily on accessibility, refining button sizes and font hierarchies.

Automation Logic

I worked on translating manual workflows into automated scripts (migrating from n8n to Python), ensuring that when a campaign status changed in the database, the client's dashboard updated instantly without manual data entry.

Copywriting

I crafted clear, concise copy for the interface, ensuring that complex financial and campaign data was presented in an understandable way for clients across different regions and languages.

Key Challenges & Solutions

Challenge

The user interface felt static and "corporate."

Solution

I implemented custom CSS animations for interactions (like button hovers and registration flows) to make the platform feel modern and responsive, debugging complex layout shifts to ensure smoothness.

Challenge

Handling complex financial data for different regions.

Solution

I designed the Finance module to handle multi-currency displays clearly, ensuring clients understood their ROI regardless of the market.

Deliver

The Impact

Efficiency

Drastically reduced the time account managers spend on weekly reporting by automating data visualization.

Client Trust

Increased transparency has led to higher client retention, as they can now see "where the money goes" in real-time.

Scalability

The modular codebase allows us to easily add new features (like AI-based influencer matching) in future sprints.

Brand Portal dashboard showcasing the unified client experience

Takeaways

Building the Brand Portal was a unique opportunity to combine product management, design, and development skills in a single project. The experience reinforced the value of wearing multiple hats—being able to translate stakeholder needs directly into code allowed for faster iteration and more user-centric solutions. The project also highlighted the importance of automation in reducing manual work and increasing transparency, which ultimately led to stronger client relationships and improved operational efficiency.
