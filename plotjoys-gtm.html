Project Overview

I led the go-to-market strategy for PlotJoys, an early-stage startup building brand awareness from zero. The challenge was to establish market presence, validate product-market fit through real user acquisition, and build a scalable growth engine on a lean budget.

Through a combination of TikTok influencer marketing, digital channel strategy, and data-driven iteration, I designed and executed a GTM plan that moved PlotJoys from concept to active user acquisition.

Project Goals

Launch PlotJoys into the market with a lean, data-driven GTM strategy. Build brand awareness from zero, validate acquisition channels, and establish a repeatable growth framework for scaling.

Discover

The Challenge

PlotJoys was an early-stage startup with zero brand awareness, no established audience, and a limited marketing budget. The challenge was to build visibility and acquire initial users in a competitive market.

Launching a new product with no existing user base requires a fundamentally different approach than scaling an established brand. Every dollar spent needs to be validated, every channel needs to prove its worth, and the strategy needs to be flexible enough to pivot based on real-time data.

Zero Brand Awareness

No existing audience, social presence, or brand recognition to leverage for initial traction.

Lean Budget

Limited marketing budget requiring maximum efficiency and careful channel selection to prove ROI.

Define

The GTM Hypothesis

TikTok influencer marketing combined with targeted digital channels would be the most cost-effective way to build awareness and validate product-market fit for PlotJoys' target demographic.

Based on audience research and competitive analysis, I hypothesized that a creator-led content strategy on TikTok would deliver the best CAC for PlotJoys' target audience. The plan combined organic influencer content for awareness with targeted paid channels for conversion, creating a full-funnel GTM approach.

TikTok-First Strategy

Leverage TikTok's organic reach and creator ecosystem for cost-effective awareness building with the target demographic.

Full-Funnel Approach

Combine influencer content for top-of-funnel awareness with paid digital channels for mid and bottom-funnel conversion.

Develop

The Execution

I designed and executed a multi-channel GTM strategy, managing influencer selection, content direction, channel mix allocation, and performance tracking across the entire campaign.

Influencer Selection

Identified and onboarded creators aligned with PlotJoys' brand values and target audience, focusing on authentic storytelling over follower count.

Content Strategy

Developed content briefs and guidelines that balanced brand messaging with creator authenticity, optimizing for TikTok's algorithm and engagement patterns.

Key Execution Areas

  • Channel Mix: Balanced organic influencer content with paid amplification across TikTok and complementary digital channels
  • Budget Optimization: Allocated budget across channels based on real-time performance data, shifting spend toward highest-performing channels
  • Performance Tracking: Built tracking infrastructure to monitor CAC, engagement rates, and conversion metrics across all channels
  • Iterative Optimization: Continuously refined targeting, creative direction, and channel allocation based on data-driven insights

Deliver

The Outcome

The GTM campaign successfully established PlotJoys' market presence and validated the acquisition strategy, providing a foundation for scaling growth.

Brand Awareness Established

Built PlotJoys' social presence and brand recognition from zero, generating organic engagement and community growth.

Channel Validation

Validated TikTok influencer marketing as the most cost-effective acquisition channel for the target audience.

Lean CAC

Achieved efficient customer acquisition costs through strategic channel allocation and continuous optimization.

Scalable Framework

Established a repeatable GTM playbook and growth framework ready to scale with increased investment.

Impact

The PlotJoys GTM campaign demonstrated that a lean, data-driven approach to market entry can build meaningful traction without massive budgets. By combining influencer authenticity with strategic paid amplification, the campaign established a validated growth model that PlotJoys can scale as the product matures. The framework and learnings from this campaign continue to inform the company's growth strategy.
