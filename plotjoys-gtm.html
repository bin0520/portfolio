Background

PlotJoys was founded by my friend Dao with a magical vision: every child deserves to be the hero of their own story. Using a proprietary AI illustration pipeline, they craft one-of-a-kind, fully personalized books.

The Challenge

The team was product-heavy (AI & engineering) but had zero marketing presence. I stepped in not just as a "friend helping out," but as the Solo Marketing Lead to build the entire Go-To-Market (GTM) infrastructure. I transitioned the company from a silent product to a brand with active customers and a validated acquisition loop.

The Multi-Channel GTM Strategy

Personalized products for children are high-trust, emotional purchases. I bypassed traditional "corporate" ads and designed a content-first, creator-led growth engine across the platforms where parents actually spend their time.

The Three-Pillar Growth Framework

The "Social Proof" Engine

TikTok & Instagram

Leveraging the "Reveal Moment" — the exact second a child sees themselves in the book — to create viral, high-conversion organic and paid content.

The "Authority" Engine

YouTube

Partnering with family vloggers and educational creators for long-form reviews to build deep trust and SEO value.

The "Retention" Engine

Email

Building automated drip campaigns to convert "browsers" into "buyers" and maximize LTV.

Develop

Full-Funnel Execution

As the sole marketer, I didn't just strategize; I executed every touchpoint.

Dominating Video Platforms (TikTok, IG & YouTube)

Instead of generic ads, I focused on Persona-Matched Creator Partnerships:

TikTok & Reels

Managed 20+ micro-influencer collaborations focusing on "edutainment" and unboxing. I handled everything from vetting creators to brief creation and performance tracking.

YouTube Integration

Secured long-form placements with parenting creators, providing an "evergreen" source of high-intent traffic.

Paid Social

Used top-performing creator content as Spark Ads (TikTok) and Partnership Ads (Meta), significantly lowering our CAC compared to standard brand creative.
