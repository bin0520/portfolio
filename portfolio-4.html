Project Overview

I orchestrated a strategic pivot for Duet's influencer campaign, moving from high-end branding to high-engagement community marketing. I oversaw the end-to-end execution, optimized the MarTech workflow for influencer selection, and managed budget allocation to maximize CAC efficiency.

Through data-driven decision making and strategic resource allocation, I directed the shift from aspirational professionals to relatable micro-influencers, establishing a validated growth model that delivered significantly improved engagement rates and more authentic brand connections.

Project Goals

Improve audience engagement rates by identifying the most effective influencer persona for the target demographic, while optimizing cost efficiency and content authenticity.

01

Discover

The Challenge

Initially, the brand focused on "High-End Professionals" (White-collar/Elite) influencers. However, we observed that while the content looked polished, the audience engagement (likes/comments) was low, and the content felt "distant" and lacked relatability.

The polished, professional content created by high-end influencers was visually appealing but failed to connect with the target audience on an emotional level. Engagement metrics showed low interaction rates, resulting in inefficient budget utilization and a disconnect between marketing spend and actual conversion intent.

02

Define

The Hypothesis

Identifying a gap in market fit, I directed the strategy towards "Authenticity over Aspiration." I aligned the team's focus on University Students & Mid-tier Creators to capture the underserved demographic and maximize ROI.

Based on engagement data and audience behavior analysis, I established a strategic direction that shifted from aspirational content to authentic, relatable storytelling. I directed the team to prioritize micro-influencers and student creators who could create more genuine connections with the target audience through everyday scenarios and authentic experiences, positioning this as a scalable approach for future campaigns.

Authenticity Over Aspiration

Real, relatable content would outperform polished professional content for this specific product and audience.

Micro-Influencer Advantage

University students and mid-tier creators (KOC) would create stronger emotional connections than high-end professionals.

03

Develop

The Experiment

I orchestrated a strategic redesign of the influencer selection process and launched a controlled pilot program with a new cohort of creators, managing the end-to-end execution and performance oversight.

Strategy Shift

Directed the shift in influencer selection criteria from "Professional Status" to "Relatability & Storytelling", establishing new evaluation frameworks.

Pilot Program Execution

Managed the recruitment and onboarding of a test group of mid-tier student creators to produce daily routine scenarios (e.g., dormitory life, study sessions).

Key Changes

  • Workflow Efficiency: Streamlined the selection process using data-driven MarTech tools, moving from manual professional status evaluation to automated relatability and storytelling ability scoring
  • Risk Management: Tested the new strategy with a controlled budget allocation first, validating the approach before full-scale deployment
  • Creator Profile: Directed focus toward university students and mid-tier creators (micro-influencers/KOC) rather than high-end professionals, optimizing for both engagement and cost efficiency
  • Content Themes: Established content guidelines emphasizing daily routines, dormitory life, study sessions, and relatable moments over polished professional settings

Impact

The strategic pivot I orchestrated led to a complete overhaul of the influencer selection process, establishing a scalable framework that delivered improved engagement metrics, better cost efficiency, and more authentic brand connections with the target demographic. This model has since been adopted as the standard approach for future campaigns.
